Project Summary: Latitude Impacts on City Weather

The intent of the project was to analyze the impact of different latitudes on various aspects of weather. For analysis, data was pulled from OpenWeatherMap API to compile a dataset of over 500 cities.


Once the dataset was complied, Matplotlib was used to plot various aspects of weather compared with city latitude. Temperature, Cloudiness, Wind Speed, and Humidity were the weather aspects included in the analysis against latitude. Included in this site are visualizations, original source data, and explanations/descriptions of trends and correlations seen.

Jupyter notebook and pandas was used to convert the csv to a dataframe and then into an html file which could be moved to the proper folder and then linked to the rest of the webpages.
